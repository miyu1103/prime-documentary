## 0. ABSOLUTE HARD RULES (R3 — read first, apply to EVERY image)

1. **NO real-person likeness. NO faces of identifiable people. EVER.** This story involves famous living
   people (actors, a fixer, coaches, parents). They must NEVER be depicted. All humans are anonymous:
   faceless, backs, torsos, hands, shoulders, silhouettes, out-of-focus, cropped above the chest or
   below the eyes, or seen from behind. No recognizable face, no celebrity, no caricature, no look-alike.
2. **NO text of any kind inside the image** — no letters, numbers, signs, logos, badges, mascots,
   university names, brand marks, watermarks, readable documents, screens, captions. Remotion adds ALL text.
3. **NO real institution identifiers** — no real university crests, gates, mascots, stadiums, or
   recognizable campus landmarks; build *archetypal* elite-campus imagery (ivy, stone, arches, quads) that
   evokes the idea without copying any real school.
4. **Symbolic reconstruction only.** Nothing may look like authentic news footage, a real photograph of a
   real event, surveillance, or a real document. These are cinematic illustrations.
5. **No real-person likeness even in "athlete photo" / "headshot" / "courtroom" beats** — fabricated
   athletic profiles are shown as faceless/obscured bodies; courtroom figures are silhouettes from behind.

If any image risks breaking rules 1–5, regenerate. A face, a logo, or a real landmark = automatic reject.

## 1. GLOBAL STYLE BIBLE (the look of every still)

- **Genre:** museum-grade cinematic symbolic documentary; "prestige true-crime / money-and-power" — the
  visual register of a feature investigative doc, not a news package and not a stock photo.
- **Palette (PD brand, strict):** deep black and midnight-navy base; **electric blue `#1F6BFF`** as the
  cold "system / surveillance / money-wire" signal; **muted gold `#E5B53A`** as the "privilege / prestige /
  temptation" accent; silver-grey highlights. Restrained, filmic contrast. No candy neon, no teal-orange.
- **Light:** always motivated (a window, a desk lamp, a phone glow, a stadium light, a courthouse
  clerestory). Low-key, directional, with real falloff and shadow. Volumetric haze where it earns depth.
  Never flat, never blown-out HDR, never muddy black-crush.
- **Texture/material:** believable stone, ivy, paper, glass, brushed metal, fabric, cash, screen glow,
  rain, dust in light. Fine cinematic film grain. Absolutely no plastic "AI sheen."
- **Composition:** one clear subject; deliberate **negative space (usually upper third or one side) left
  empty for Remotion headlines/lower-thirds**; strong depth (fore/mid/background); rule-of-thirds or
  centered symmetry as the beat calls for. Each still must work for a slow push-in, parallax, or crop.
- **Output spec:** **16:9, long edge ≥ 3840 px (3840×2160 target)**, highest quality the model allows;
  if raw gen is smaller, it will be upscaled — compose with that crop room.
- **Recurring visual motifs (use deliberately, they thread the film):** THREE DOORS (a grand front door,
  a hidden back door, a small unmarked **side door**); the empty chair / single open seat; the ladder and
  the missing rung; gold thread vs. a cold blue ledger; a fabricated photo on a corkboard; the manila
  envelope; the wire-transfer light traveling along a line; the campus gate at dawn.

## 3. QUALITY BAR (a still is rejected unless ALL pass)

- Clear subject hierarchy + intentional empty space for text overlays; mobile-readable focal point.
- Motivated, controlled lighting; cinematic depth; no flat stock look; no black crush.
- Believable materials + fine grain; no plastic sheen; no anatomy errors.
- Narrative duty: the image must locate, explain, humanize, symbolize, or build tension for its exact beat.
- Safety: no face/likeness, no logo/text, no real landmark, not authentic-footage-looking.
- Editability: parallax/Ken-Burns friendly, crop room, does not fight captions/lower-thirds.
- Neighbor fit: distinct from adjacent shots (angle, scale, brightness, or subject must change).
