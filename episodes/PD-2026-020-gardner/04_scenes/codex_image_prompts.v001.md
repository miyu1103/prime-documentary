## 0. ABSOLUTE HARD RULES (R2 — read first, apply to EVERY image)
1. **NO real-person face or likeness of anyone.** People are anonymous: from behind, cropped, silhouettes,
   hands only, out of focus. No look-alikes of any suspect, guard, official, or artist.
2. **NO real Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum** interior, exterior, courtyard, logo, or identifiable architecture.
   Build an ARCHETYPAL old-master gallery (dark paneled rooms, parquet, ornate gilded frames) that evokes the
   idea without copying the real place.
3. **NO authentic reproductions of the 13 stolen artworks.** Never render a recognizable copy of the Vermeer,
   the Rembrandts, the Manet, the Degas, etc. Represent them as EMPTY gilded frames, draped/cut canvases,
   covered easels, or abstract suggestions (a stormy-sea shape, a music-scene silhouette) — clearly not the real work.
4. **NO text** inside the image — no letters, numbers, signs, logos, watermarks, readable documents/screens.
5. **Symbolic reconstruction only** — nothing looks like authentic 1990 footage, surveillance, or a real photo.
A face, the real museum, a real artwork copy, a real logo, or readable text = automatic reject and regenerate.

## 1. GLOBAL STYLE BIBLE
- **Genre:** museum-grade cinematic symbolic documentary; "prestige unsolved-mystery / art-heist noir."
- **Palette (PD brand, strict):** deep black + midnight-navy base; **electric blue `#1F6BFF`** as the cold
  "investigation / night / forensic" signal; **muted gold `#E5B53A`** as the "art / value / the empty frame"
  accent; silver-grey highlights. Restrained filmic contrast. No candy neon, no teal-orange.
- **Light:** motivated and low-key — a single guard's flashlight, a sodium streetlight, moonlight through tall
  windows, a desk lamp, a gallery picture-light. Real falloff, deep shadow, volumetric haze where it earns depth.
- **Texture/material:** aged gilt, cracked varnish, parquet, velvet rope, brass, canvas weave, duct tape, rain
  on glass, dust in a light beam. Fine cinematic grain. No plastic AI sheen.
- **Composition:** one clear subject; deliberate negative space for Remotion text; strong fore/mid/background
  depth; works for slow push, parallax, or crop.
- **Output:** 16:9, long edge **>= 3840 px (3840x2160)**, highest quality; compose with upscale crop room.
- **Recurring motifs:** the EMPTY GILDED FRAME; the cut canvas edge; two uniformed silhouettes; the night
