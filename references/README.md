# References — rights-clean source material

Materials we are ALLOWED to use, downloaded for grounding and (where noted) for the videos.

## ✅ Usable now (in this folder)
- `opinions/*.txt` — **U.S. Supreme Court opinion text**. These are works of the U.S. government and are
  **public domain** — safe to keep, quote on screen (short, attributed), and use to ground claims.
  Source URLs are recorded at the top of each file.
  - terry_392US1_1968, riley_573US373_2014, carpenter_585US296_2018, timbs_586US146_2019, kelo_545US469_2005
    (kelo file is the syllabus; full opinion can be added if needed).

## ✅ Other rights-clean material we may use in the videos (not yet downloaded — say the word)
- **Oral-argument audio** from the Supreme Court / Oyez — official SCOTUS recordings are **public domain**;
  real argument audio is a strong, authentic in-video asset. Heavy media → store on `H:\pd-media`, not the repo.
- **Public-domain photographs / government images** (e.g., federal agency photos, NARA) — usable with attribution.
- **Our own generated assets** — Codex / SDXL images and Suno music are ours to use.

## ❌ NOT usable (avoid — copyright / Content ID / ban risk)
- Copyrighted news footage, photos, or TV clips of the cases or people.
- Identifiable real-person likenesses generated by AI (invariant 11).
Use only the rights-clean material above; see docs/13 (rights, safety & publish risk).
